Description

Text is the form of data which has existed for millenniums throughout the human history. All the sacred texts influencing all the religions, all the compositions of poets and authors, all the scientific explanations by the brightest minds of their times, all the political documents which define our history and our future, and all kind of explicit human communication, these "All"s define the importance of data available in the form of what we call text.
Data scientists spend most of their time not on modeling but on cleaning and exploring the data. Furthermore, different approaches in text cleaning can lead to very diverse results during model training.
